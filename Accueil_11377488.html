Pangea

the experience of a century

Go anywhere...

​ With Pangea, you can go where you’ve never been, all the while staying at home! Are you dreaming about visiting Paris, New-York, or Tokyo? You only need to launch Pangea.  In case you already live in a city, you can have a change of scenery and opt for the vast wilderness! You can choose to travel to Iceland, to Yosemite park, or to the African Savanna, to name but a few examples! 

Why not sail across the universe? From Halley’s Comet to the Moon, with stops on Mercury, Mars, Jupiter, or the Pillars of Creation, many celestial objects are available on Pangaea. . All the known places in the universe are available on Pangaea.

See everything...

Who has never dreamed of rewinding the clock? This option allows you to learn a lot about our past, especially cultural aspects and building’s architectures. From Antiquity to World War Two, not to mention the French Renaissance or the Roaring Twenties, you will meet history and learn more about these interesting times.

Another option, which from my point of view is the most exciting one, especially if you are a film-lover, is that you can explore wonderful fictional worlds. You can swim in the chocolate pool of Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, take part in the ring’s adventure with Frodo, or become the future Toruk Makto. Anyway, with the film-makers’ permission, you’re free to do whatever you want in these fictional worlds! 

... for everyone

​ If you are blind or visually deficient, no problem! We have implemented an AI that creates audio descriptions. It describes the visual elements of the scene through an elaborate and revolutionary protocol.

If you are deaf or hearing impaired, no problem either! Our AI also includes real-time subtitles and an avatar that can communicate with you in sign language.

In any case, the app is easy and intuitive to understand.
